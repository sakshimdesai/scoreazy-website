Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our mentorship program.

How long is each mentorship session and how often do they occur? +

Each session is 60 minutes long, providing ample time for in-depth discussion, problem-solving, and progress review. Sessions typically occur weekly, but can be adjusted based on your needs and academic schedule.

What subjects and grade levels do your mentors cover? +

Our mentors specialize in all major subjects including Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English, Social Studies, and competitive exam preparation for grades 6-12 across CBSE, ICSE, and State boards.

Is there a minimum commitment period for the program? +

While we recommend a minimum of 3 months to see significant improvement, you can start with our flexible monthly plans. Most students continue for 6-12 months to achieve their long-term academic goals.

How do you track and measure student progress? +

We use a comprehensive tracking system with regular assessments, progress reports, goal milestone tracking, and detailed parent updates. You'll receive monthly progress reports and can track improvement in real-time.

What makes your approach different from regular tutoring? +

Unlike traditional tutoring, we use educational psychology principles to understand how each student learns best. Our holistic approach focuses on building study skills, confidence, and learning strategies alongside subject mastery.
